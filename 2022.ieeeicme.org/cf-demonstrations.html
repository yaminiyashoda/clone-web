Call for Demonstrations

Demonstrations are called for ICME 2022, which can be either an independent one-page demo paper or associated with a paper from ICME 2022 main program and/or co-located workshops.

Requirements

For one-page demo paper, the submission should follow the guidelines available on the conference website. The goal of the Demo Papers Program is to promote applied research and applications along with the Expo Program, as well as facilitate collaborations between industrial and academic members of the multimedia community. Topics of interest may span all ICME 2022 areas. A prospective paper should provide comprehensive descriptions on the technology to be demonstrated, including the equipment involved and/or setting-ups necessary for attendants to follow up an approach or system. Demo submission can be accompanied with multimedia attachments of up to 100 MB.

Note that an accepted demo paper will be included in the proceedings and appear in IEEE Xplore.
